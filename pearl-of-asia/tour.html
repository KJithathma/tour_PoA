Pearl of Asia Tour

INTRODUCING OUR BEST TOURS

Highland Rail Escape - 04 days

Colombo → Kandy → Ella

Experience Sri Lanka’s most scenic train journey from Kandy to Ella, passing lush tea plantations and misty mountains.

Southern Adventure

Colombo → Galle → Yala → Nuwara Eliya

Experience Sri Lanka’s weather extremes in one day! Enjoy Galle’s sunny beaches, Yala’s wildlife, and Nuwara Eliya’s cool tea-covered hills.

Cultural Triangle

Colombo → Kandy → Sigiriya

Step into Sri Lanka’s rich history by visiting Kandy’s sacred Temple of the Tooth, the breathtaking Sigiriya Rock Fortress, and the surrounding ancient ruins. This trip offers a perfect mix of cultural exploration, scenic beauty, and local experiences.

Ancient Kingdoms Tour

Colombo → Kandy → Sigiriya → Anuradhapura → Polonnaruwa

Discover the ancient capitals of Kandy, Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, and Polonnaruwa, exploring temples, ruins, and sacred sites.

Coastal Getaway

Colombo → Mirissa

Whether by train or bus, head to Mirissa for a relaxing beach escape. Enjoy whale watching, stunning sunsets, and golden sands, making it the perfect destination for those seeking a tropical retreat filled with adventure and relaxation.

Heritage & Beach

Colombo → Dambulla → Polonnaruwa → Pasikuda

Begin journey exploring Sri Lanka’s cultural gems,including the Dambulla Cave Temple and the ancient city of Polonnaruwa.Then,unwind in Pasikuda, famous for its calm turquoise waters and pristine beaches, offering the perfect blend of history and coastal beauty.

Ultimate Beach Trail

Colombo → Unawatuna → Hikkaduwa → Thalpe → Mirissa → Hiriketiya → Blue Beach Island → Hambantota → Arugam Bay → Kalmunai → Trincomalee

Hop between Sri Lanka’s best beaches, from Unawatuna to Arugam Bay, for surf, sun, and stunning coastal views.

Misty Hills Retreat

Colombo → Idalgashinna

Escape to Idalgashinna’s peaceful hills, hike through tea plantations, and enjoy breathtaking mountain views.
